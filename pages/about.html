🏋️ About Us

We Build Tools.
You Build Gyms.

HomeGymCalc exists because building a home gym shouldn't require a spreadsheet, a degree in exercise science, and 40 hours of YouTube research.

Why We Built This

When we started building home gyms, the advice online fell into two camps: vague blog posts that recommended everything, and brand-sponsored content that pushed whatever paid the highest affiliate rate.

Neither was actually useful. We wanted tools — calculators that took our specific situation (apartment, $800, goal: lose weight) and gave us a concrete answer.

So we built them. HomeGymCalc is a collection of free, data-driven tools that give you honest recommendations based on what you tell us — not what any brand pays us to say.
