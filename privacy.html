Privacy Policy

Calculator inputs: When you use our calculators, inputs you enter (budget, goals, etc.) are processed in your browser and sent to our API to generate recommendations. We do not store your calculator inputs tied to your identity.

Email address: If you choose to subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address and store it in Mailchimp. You can unsubscribe at any time.

Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand traffic patterns. This collects anonymized data including pages visited, time spent, and general location (country/region level). No personally identifiable information is collected.
